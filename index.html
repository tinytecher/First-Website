Hi, I'm Nid Noi

My name means 'little bit' in Thai.

I'm a winter white Hamster and this website is all about me!

I'm known by a few other names, such as the Russian Dwarf Hamster or the Siberian Hamster. I'm approximately 7 to 9 centimeters long, I live for around 1 to 3 years and have an awesome racing stripe down my back

Im called a Winter White because my fur can turn pure white in the winter.

In the wild you would normally find me in Kazakhstan, Mongolia, Siberia and Manchuria.
