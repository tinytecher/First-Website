What I Eat

Now one of my favorite topics, food!, For a balanced diet We love a mix of grasses, seeds and grains , along with small amounts of vegtables. We are also omnivores and need protein, so feel free to add in things like mealworms, chicken or egg into our diet as well

As far as sweet treats go, we can only have tiny amounts as we are very prone to dieabeties, so anything sugary should be kept to an absolute minimum!

Treats are also a big deal for us and we could happily ingore everything else and munch on sunflower seeds and peanuts all day but this would'nt be very good for us so dont give us to many.

I might be alowed more treats after my last weight in as my ideal weight should between 30 to 45 grams when i'm fully grown, no diet for me just yet!

If you want a more detailed list of what we can and can't eat take a look at this guide on Hamster Hideout; Hamster Hideout Food Guide
